## Gotchas this lap (also in PENDING_WORK)
- `f (Fin.cons a w i)` in a self-written statement fails elaboration (motive
  metavar) — ascribe `(Fin.cons a w : Fin (n+1) → α) i`.
- `ENNReal.tsum_eq_add_tsum_ite` bakes in `Classical.propDecidable`; your own
  `if b = 3` uses `instDecidableEqNat` — bridge via `by_cases <;> simp [hx]`.
- Backward `rw [← h]` with `h : a + b = 4` also rewrites the `4` inside `4⁻¹` —
  use `.trans h.symm` + `ENNReal.add_right_inj`.
- `refine … (by dsimp only; omega)` dies with "No goals" when unification made
  the goal rfl; `(by dsimp only <;> omega)` is vacuous-safe.
- When a fold-step state's barrier is a `coveringTriangle` expression, DON'T
  write the record literal: `set σ' := encStep …`, prove the field equations
  (`σ'.count = 1` etc. via `rw [encStep, dif_pos hq']`), and keep the barrier
  abstract — consumers quantify over it anyway.
- `rw [if_pos (by omega)]` with two `ite`s in the goal can aim the metavar
  condition at the wrong one — always `if_pos (show _ by …)` with the explicit
  condition.
