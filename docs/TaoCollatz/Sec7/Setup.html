    def TaoCollatz.sfrac (q : ℚ) :
    ℚ

    Signed fractional part q - round q, valued in [-1/2, 1/2) (mathlib round is half-UP). The paper's {·} lands in (-1/2, 1/2] instead; the two conventions differ only at exact half-integers, which the phases here never attain (their denominators are odd powers of 3, so θ ≡ 1/2 (mod 1) is impossible) — ratified, 2026-07-09 judge pass.

    Equations
    • TaoCollatz.sfrac q = q - ↑(round q)
