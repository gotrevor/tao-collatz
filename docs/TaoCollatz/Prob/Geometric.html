    Geometric / Pascal PMFs and the negative binomial (node S2) #

    Paper anchors: Tao 2019 Def 1.7, §2.

    • geomHalf — Geom(2): P(a) = 2⁻ᵃ, a ≥ 1.
    • geomQuarter — Geom(4)-shaped: P(a) = 4⁻¹·(3/4)^(a-1), a ≥ 1.
    • pascal — law of a₁+a₂ for two independent Geom(2): P(b) = (b-1)·2⁻ᵇ, b ≥ 2.
    • pascalNe3 — pascal conditioned to avoid b = 3 (§7 pairing).

    All four normalization proofs (HasSum … 1) are proved: geometric sums for geomHalf/geomQuarter; for pascal the ∑ n·rⁿ closed form over ℝ lifted through ℝ≥0 to ℝ≥0∞; pascalNe3 by splitting off the b = 3 atom.

    -- RATIFY-DRIFT: pascal/pascalNe3 spell the coefficient ((b - 1 : ℕ) : ℝ≥0∞) -- (ℕ-subtraction, then cast) rather than the spec's ambiguous (b - 1) (which Lean -- would read as ENNReal truncated subtraction ↑b - 1). The two agree for every -- unmasked b ≥ 2, so the mathematical content is identical.
